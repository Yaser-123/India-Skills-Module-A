A2 :- Day/Night Sky Cycle
The Core Functions/Features of this above project:-
1. Night: Dark sky with visible moon, stars and cloud
2. Dawn/Sunrise: sky brightens from the horizon upward
3. Day: full daylight with sun at peak position
4. Dusk / Sunset: sun descends and sky transotopns back to night
